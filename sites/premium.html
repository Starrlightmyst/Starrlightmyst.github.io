Our Special Collection

Unique, high-quality printable planners and goodies with a focus on goals, motivation, task completion, and personal development. Each package includes high-resolution 300dpi A5, A4, B5, Letter, and Half Letter sized versions for easy use.

Features

Professional

At 300 dpi high-resolution, all premium printables feature crisp, sharp computer crafted graphics and text for a perfect professional look.

5 Sizes

Premium printables come in a set of 5 different sizes: A4, Letter, B5, A5, and Half Letter. Not just stretched to fit, but reformatted for best usage.

Margins

Designed with ultimate usability in mind, all premium prints have a .5" margin on both the left and right side ready for hole punching or binding.
